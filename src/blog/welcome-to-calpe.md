Welcome! We're so glad you're considering **Casa Perlamar** for your trip to Calpe. This little corner of the Costa Blanca has been a favourite of ours for years, and we've set up the apartment to be a comfortable, easy base for exploring it.

## A few things to look forward to

- Mornings on the **sea-view terrace** with a coffee
- A short walk to the **beach** and the marina
- Hiking the **Peñón de Ifach** for unforgettable views
- Long, lazy dinners of fresh seafood in the **old town**

## Planning your stay

Have a look at the [local area guide](/local-area/) for getting here and the best beaches, and our [recommendations](/recommendations/) for where to eat and what to see. When you're ready, send us your dates on the [contact page](/contact/) and we'll be in touch.

Buen viaje — we hope to welcome you soon.
